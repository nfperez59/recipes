Strawberry Pies

by Nicolas Perez

Ingredients

  • 1 Ready Crust Reduced Fat Graham Cracker Pie Crust
  • 1 pkg (4 serving size) sugar free jello (Strawberry, Key Lime etc.)
  • 1/4 cup boiling water
  • 2 containers (6oz) Lite Greek Yogurt (Strawberry, Key Lime, etc.)
  • 1 container (8 oz) fat free whipped topping, thawed

Directions

In large bowl, dissolve jello in boiling water. Stir in yogurt with wire whisk. Fold in whipped topping. Spread in crust. Refrigerate at least 2 hours or overnight.

Recipe should be either 3 or 5 Weight Watchers points, depending on which system you use (old or new). You can use any flavor jello or Greek yogurt that you want.
