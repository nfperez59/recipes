Vegan Broccoli Soup

by Lauramarie Laskey

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, peeled and diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups broccoli florets (about 1 head of broccoli)
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1/4 tsp thyme
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instruction

  1. In a large stockpot, heat the olive oil over medium heat and cook the onions and garlic until tender.
  2. Add the vegetable broth and potatoes to the pot. Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce back to medium heat and cook until potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes.
  3. Add the broccoli, spinach, and thyme, and cover for 5 minutes, until fully steamed but still bright green.
  4. Puree the soup in a blender or with a stick blender. Season with salt and pepper and serve with chives and/or sour cream (vegan or non-vegan), if desired.
